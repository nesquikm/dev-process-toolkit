## Requirement

`specs/INDEX.md` is a generated write-only artifact. `/implement` Phase 4, `/spec-write` step 0b.5, and `/spec-archive` steps 4+5 all regenerate it; nothing reads past entries. Humans can browse `specs/frs/*.md` directly and Linear's UI is the authoritative active-FR index. Post M12-M16 archival the file is empty. Delete the file, the `regenerateIndex` helper, and all writer call sites.

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC-STE-57.1: `specs/INDEX.md` is deleted from the repo.
- AC-STE-57.2: The `regenerateIndex(specsDir)` helper function is deleted from its current location (`plugins/dev-process-toolkit/adapters/_shared/src/index_gen.ts` or equivalent — ripgrep `regenerateIndex` to locate) along with its test file.
- AC-STE-57.3: All `regenerateIndex` call sites are removed from skill prose:
  - `skills/implement/SKILL.md` Phase 4 archival step
  - `skills/spec-write/SKILL.md` step 0b.5
  - `skills/spec-archive/SKILL.md` steps 4 and 5
- AC-STE-57.4: Docs updated to remove `INDEX.md` references:
  - `docs/v2-layout-reference.md` — INDEX.md sections removed
  - `docs/sdd-methodology.md` (line 35 tree diagram, line 49 archival narrative)
  - `docs/skill-anatomy.md` (line 93 pattern note)
  - `docs/adaptation-guide.md` (line 240 archival narrative)
  - `docs/patterns.md` (lines 338, 343 — Archival Lifecycle pattern)
  - `docs/implement-reference.md` (line 52 archival step)

- AC-STE-57.6: `specs/frs/archive/index.md` and `specs/plan/archive/index.md` (per-unit archive markers) are **explicitly preserved** — they serve a different purpose (archive-scoped listings) with separate lifecycle.

## Technical Design

Subtractive. Archival simplifies after this FR: FRs archive via `git mv` + frontmatter flip, no index regeneration step. The per-unit archive indices (`archive/index.md` under `frs/` and `plan/`) are separate files with separate purpose and stay.

## Testing

`index_gen.test.ts` and any `regenerateIndex`-specific tests deleted. Archival tests must continue to pass without the regeneration step.

## Notes

Independent of other FRs. Can land in parallel with anything. FR-C1's deletion of `docs/setup-migrate.md` removes one of FR-C3's doc-update sites — if FR-C1 lands first, FR-C3 has one less file to touch (no conflict either way).

The per-unit archive index files (`archive/index.md` under `frs/` and `plan/`) are intentionally preserved. They are different from the root `INDEX.md`: they are archive-scoped directories-of-contents, generated at archival time, and not regenerated by `/implement`, `/spec-write`, or `/spec-archive` on the active path.
