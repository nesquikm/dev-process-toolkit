**Goal:** spec-write flow determinism, two legs. **(1) Next-line recommendation:** `/spec-write`'s
closing `Next:` line recommends `/dev-process-toolkit:implement M<N>` for milestone-bound FRs
instead of unconditionally recommending the single-FR form — the current rule (STE-181
AC-STE-181.3) keys on new-FR presence, pointing the operator away from the Phase 5 milestone close;
new discriminator: **milestone binding**, guarded by doc-shape probe #66 so the paragraph can't
silently regress the way the STE-181 shape ossified. **(2) Kickoff branch type:** the freestyle

across sessions) is replaced by the deterministic `branchTypeFor` rule keyed on `changelog_category`
frontmatter (`Fixed`/`Security` ⇒ `fix`, else `feat`; `--no-tech` ⇒ `chore`), consumed by both
`/spec-write` § 7a and `/implement` 0.b″. Both legs approved 2026-07-14 `/brainstorm`.
