## M97: Milestone-label coverage {#M97}

**Goal:** Close the two holes that let an FR-backed tracker ticket ship without its `milestone-<M>` binding (Jira `milestone-M<N>` label / Linear native project milestone). Root cause found 2026-07-03 on `../st/glacy-app-be` (Jira): **GB-11** (Story, M5) shipped with no `milestone-M5` label because the `/spec-write` milestone-attach is non-blocking (spec is source of truth, no rollback on `MilestoneAttachmentError`), the `/implement` § 0.e re-attach net did not durably land it, and once the FR archived, `/gate-check` probe #26 (`tracker-project-milestone-attached`) went vacuous and never re-flagged it. **Option 2** (2026-07-03 `/brainstorm`): enforce milestone bindings on **FR-backed** tickets only — **GB-12** (a Bug with no FR) is intentionally out of scope, since bugs/ad-hoc issues are not milestone-scoped. Three moves: reliable landing at attach time (FR-1), an archival-time assertion so a gap can't hide behind an archived FR (FR-2), and a one-shot backfill for already-shipped gaps (FR-3).

**Prerequisites:** None new. Builds on `adapters/_shared/src/attach_project_milestone.ts` (attach + read-back verify + `planFileHeadingToMilestoneName`), `/spec-write` § milestone-attach, `/implement` § 0.e + Phase 4 close, `/spec-archive`, `/gate-check` probe #26, and the `upsertTicketMetadata` Gateway-Timeout `1s+2s+4s` backoff pattern.
